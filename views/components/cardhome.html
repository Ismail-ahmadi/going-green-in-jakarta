The Jakarta Green List

Eat, live, and breathe green. We're making a list of all the green things in Jakarta that we find.

Eat Green
Green Groceries: Buy your groceries #plasticfree. Find organic, natural and home-grown veggies.
Shop Green
Earth conscious consumers: Environmentally-friendly products for everything from home cleaning, fashion, personal hygiene, and more.
Green Leisure
Family-friendly fun with nature: Parks, holiday spots, places to stay, green spaces in the city
Recycling & Composting
Responsible waste disposal: The people and places that give second life to your household paper, plastic, glass, batteries etc.
Dine Green
Restaurants & Cafes: Vegan, vegetarian, organic and #plasticfree options
Green Partners
Organisations, Campaigns and Networks: Connect with organisations making a difference for an environmentally sustainable future
